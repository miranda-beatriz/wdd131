Life with Music

Reason:

This name represents what the site brings because it's not a site of a music school but a site to show how lives can be better with music and teach some general things for those who are curious.

Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide information about music and some instruments and their families (strings, woodwinds, brass, percussion).

Scenarios:

  • How is sheet music read?
  • How are instruments divided?
  • What can learning an instrument bring to life if I start today?
