Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo - Brasil

Mogi das Cruzes is a city located in the eastern part of São Paulo state, Brazil, approximately 40 kilometers (25 miles) northeast of São Paulo city. It covers an area of about 713 square kilometers (275 square miles). The city was founded on August 1, 1560, by Jesuit priests, and its name "Mogi das Cruzes" comes from the Tupi-Guarani language, meaning river of snakes or river of serpents.
